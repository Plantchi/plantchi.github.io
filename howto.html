How to play Plantchi

  • Plantchi is a multiplayer collaborative plant care game! You can take care of a plant by yourself or with friends.
  • To start, choose a plant type and you will be escorted to where the plant is.
  • Players can choose which “action” to take to care of their plant. The needs are Hunger, Thirst, Entertainment, Light.
  • You will see a bar for each need, and if the need level gets kind of low, a warning will display.
  • If the need bar gets too low, the plant will leave and the game is over.
